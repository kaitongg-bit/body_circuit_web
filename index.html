Solo Mode (1 Person)

  1. Select "Solo Mode" and click "Start"
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Raise both hands above your shoulders
  4. Stay still to close the circuit and start music
  5. Keep very still to unlock all 3 audio layers

Multi-Person Mode

  1. Select "Multi-Person Mode" and choose number of people
  2. Click "Start" and allow camera access
  3. Stand in a line facing the camera
  4. All raise hands above shoulders
  5. Hold hands to form a circuit (right hand to next person's left hand)
  6. Stay connected to keep the music playing
